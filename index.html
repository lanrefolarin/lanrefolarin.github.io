Selected Work

Projects & Case Studies

Real problems, structured thinking, measurable outcomes.

Government · Current 01
Process & Reporting · Ontario Ministry of Health
Transfer Payment Funding Package (TPFP)
User Need Program staff across Finance and Operations had no single source of truth — they were making funding allocation decisions on stale spreadsheets and hoping nothing had changed.
Decision Prioritized visibility before automation. Built the reporting layer first so leadership could trust the data before we changed any behavior. Sequenced 40+ epics by user dependency, not technical convenience.
Impact Eliminated 3 manual handoff steps. Teams stopped asking "where are we?" and started asking "what's next?" — the shift from reactive to planned delivery was visible within the first sprint cycle.
Government · Current 02
Financial Planning · Ontario Ministry of Health
Planning, Budgeting & Forecasting Transformation
User Need Finance teams were making multi-million dollar planning decisions on data they didn't fully trust. Errors in ERP datasets were silently propagating into forecasts — and nobody could pinpoint where.
Decision Fixed the problem at the source, not the symptom. Defined validation rules at data entry rather than building error-correction layers downstream — a harder short-term lift, but a fraction of the ongoing cost.
Impact Data errors dropped ~25% within 60 days. The real win: forecast reports went from "we'll double-check that" to trusted artifacts that leadership acted on without hesitation.
Telecom · Previous 03
Cost Optimization · Bell & Telus
Copper Decommissioning Program
User Need Finance teams couldn't dispute contractor invoices or verify material recovery — $2M+ was being reconciled on trust and approximation, with no audit trail if something went wrong.
Decision Held the line on traceability over speed. Delayed releases twice to enforce full RTM sign-off — because shipping broken financial data would've cost more to fix than to prevent. Not everyone agreed, but the outcome proved the call.
Impact Zero-defect releases across both major delivery cycles. Reconciliation effort dropped 40%. Finance teams could now challenge contractor claims with data — for the first time in the program's history.

Logistics · Previous 04
Analytics Product · DB Schenker
BI Analytics Platform & CRM Implementation
User Need Operations and Finance teams were making fulfilment decisions on gut feel. Dashboards existed — but the data was unreliable enough that no one fully trusted what they showed.
Decision Enforced a zero critical defects policy and delayed go-live twice rather than ship bad data to executives. Profiled 5M+ records and applied an 80/20 prioritization — fixing the 20% of data fields driving 80% of decisions first.
Impact All 24 dashboards launched clean. On-time shipment performance improved 15% in two quarters — not because the data changed, but because teams finally trusted it enough to act on it.
FMCG · Previous 05
Process Automation · Mother Parker's Tea & Coffee
CRM & Order Automation Initiative
User Need Order errors were creating downstream chaos across 3 departments — but no single team owned the problem. Everyone felt the pain; nobody had the mandate to fix it.
Decision Ran pain-point workshops before touching scope. Refused to define requirements until the root cause was understood. Chose phased delivery over big-bang launch specifically to manage adoption risk — 50+ users is a culture change, not just a software change.
Impact Order errors down 30%. All 4 releases shipped on schedule. Full adoption within 30 days of each go-live — because the people using the system helped shape what got built.
