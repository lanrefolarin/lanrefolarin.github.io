REQ-02 / SELECTED WORK

Projects & Case Studies

Real problems, structured thinking, measurable outcomes. Each one framed the way I actually work: need, decision, impact.

Government · Current 01
Approvals Analytics & Reporting · Ontario Ministry of Health
Transfer Payment Funding Package (TPFP)
User Need Leadership had no reliable view of how Transfer Payment approval packages were moving. Status lived across inboxes and spreadsheets, so nobody could say with confidence where a package was stuck or which stage was slowest.
Decision Built the visibility layer before touching the process. Modeled the full approval pipeline by status and by approver level, and derived a clean division dimension the source system never captured so bottlenecks could be traced to where they actually happened. Sequenced 40+ epics by user dependency, and labeled known data-quality gaps openly rather than presenting shaky numbers as fact.
Impact Eliminated 3 manual handoff steps and gave leadership a single trusted view of pipeline health and cycle time. Teams stopped asking "where are we?" and started asking "what's next?". The shift from reactive to planned delivery was visible within the first sprint cycle.
Government · Current 02
Financial Planning · Ontario Ministry of Health
Planning, Budgeting & Forecasting Transformation
User Need Finance teams were making multi-million dollar planning decisions on data they didn't fully trust. ERP errors were silently compounding into forecasts with no way to trace them back to the source.
Decision Fixed the problem at the source, not the symptom. Defined validation rules and exception-handling logic at data entry rather than building error-correction layers downstream, and documented data lineage and control checkpoints so every figure could be traced back to its origin. A harder short-term lift, a fraction of the ongoing cost.
Impact Within 60 days, forecast reports went from "we'll double-check that" to trusted artifacts leadership acted on without hesitation. Data errors dropped by roughly 25%, and the team stopped building in manual verification as a default step.
Telecom · Previous 03
Cost Optimization · Combined Metal Industries (Clients: Bell & Telus)
Copper Decommissioning Program
User Need As Bell and Telus phased out copper infrastructure, Finance teams at Combined Metal Industries couldn't dispute contractor invoices or verify material recovery. $2M+ was being reconciled on trust and approximation, with no audit trail if something went wrong.
Decision Held the line on traceability over speed. Delayed releases twice to enforce full RTM sign-off because shipping broken financial data would have cost more to fix than to prevent. Not everyone agreed, but the outcome proved the call.
Impact For the first time in the program's history, Finance teams could challenge contractor claims with actual data. Zero critical defects across both major delivery cycles. Reconciliation effort dropped 40% because the audit trail that should have existed from day one finally did.

Analytics Product · DB Schenker
BI Analytics Platform & CRM Implementation
User Need Operations and Finance teams were making fulfilment decisions on gut feel, not because dashboards didn't exist but because nobody trusted what they showed.
